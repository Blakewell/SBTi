get_report(self, company)¶

Get the emissions and the temperature score given a company. Throws a CompanyNotFoundException if the company is not found. For testing purposes “Ortec Finance Data Analytics B.V.” won’t be found.

Parameters

company – str: The identifier of the company to get the emissions for

Returns

float, float: The emissions and the temperature score
